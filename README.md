# End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard
This project is a comprehensive analysis of the performance of a Super Store across various dimensions such as **Sales**, **Profit**, **Product Categories**, **Customer Segments**, and **Regional Performance**. The visualisations provide key insights that can guide strategic decision-making in areas like product focus, customer segmentation, discounting strategies, and operational improvements.

## üîç Key Insights

### 1. **Regional Powerhouses: East Leads, South Struggles**
- **East** is the top performer with **$0.59M** in sales, while the **South** struggles with a **loss of $0.01M**.
- **Central** holds its own, contributing **$0.45M** in sales.
- A deep dive into the **South** region is suggested to explore operational inefficiencies or customer dissatisfaction.

![Regional Dashboard](https://github.com/Josh-Osas/End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Regional%20Dashboard.png)

### 2. **Customer Insights: Central's Stronghold**
- **Central** has the largest customer base with **323** customers, representing **28.6%** of the total.
- **Corporate** customers lead in sales, contributing **$0.66M** in sales and **$0.05M** in profit. These customers should be prioritised for retention and expansion.

![Customer Dashboard](https://github.com/Josh-Osas/End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Customers.png)

### 3. **Sales Trends: A Seasonal Story**
- **April** saw the highest sales at **$384K**, while sales dropped in **May** to **$277K**, signaling a seasonal dip.
- Order volume also dropped from **369 orders** in **January** to **305 orders** in **June**. Strategies to maintain sales during off-peak months should be considered.

![Sales & Orders Dashboard](https://github.com/Josh-Osas/End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Sales%20%26%20Orders%20Dashboard.png)

### 4. **Profit and Discounts: Efficiency and Strategy**
- The **discounting strategy** showed a steady decline from **$17.1K** in **January** to **$15.4K** in **June**, signaling a decrease in promotional expenses.
- **Profit** increased significantly from **April to June**, reaching **$67K**. This suggests the discounting strategy is aligned with profitability goals.

![Profit & Discount Dashboard](https://github.com/Josh-Osas/End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Profit%20%26%20Discount%20Dashboard.png)

### 5. **Product Performance: Technology Shines**
- **Technology** is the highest-performing category, contributing **$0.71M** in sales and **$0.08M** in profit.
- **Office Supplies** and **Furniture** contribute less, but focus on **Technology** could drive further growth.
- **Regular Air** is the preferred method across most regions.

![Product Dashboard](https://github.com/Josh-Osas/End-to-End-Business-Performance-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/Product%20Dashboard.png)

### 7. **The Return Story: A Need for Attention**
- The **West** and **East** regions have the most returned orders.
- This points to potential quality issues, delivery challenges, or customer dissatisfaction. Investigating these areas is necessary to reduce returns and improve customer retention.

## üí° Recommendations
- **Focus on Corporate Clients**: Given their significant contribution to sales and profit, strategies should be built around retaining and growing the corporate segment.
- **Address South Region Profitability**: Investigate the factors leading to negative profit in the South and implement corrective actions.
- **Expand Technology Product Line**: Given its high sales performance, exploring additional products in this category could drive further growth.
- **Optimise Delivery Methods**: Enhancing delivery speed, especially in **East** and **West** regions, could help increase customer satisfaction and sales.
